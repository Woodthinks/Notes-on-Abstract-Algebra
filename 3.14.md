### VI Cosets and Lagrange's theorem

Recall how normal subgroups are defined:
$$
N\trianglelefteq G:
\begin{cases}
    N \leq G \\
    \forall h\in N, \forall g\in G, ghg^{-1}\in N
\end{cases}
$$
Cosets $\Rightarrow$ Equivalence classes: $H\leq G \Rightarrow \bar a = aH, G\bigsqcup$
